Ping Pong Rules & Regulations

1. The Objective
Score by hitting the ball onto the opponent's side within bounds.
2. Scoring
Earn a point when the opponent misses or hits the ball out.
3. Serving
Toss the ball 16 cm and strike it behind the end line; it must bounce on both sides.
4. Rally
Alternate hits until one player fails to return the ball.
5. Let Serve
Serve hits net but lands correctly — replay the point.
6. Game & Match
Games to 11 points. Win by 2. Matches are best of 5 or 7.
7. Equipment
Paddle, ball, and standard 2.74m x 1.525m table.
8. Faults
Bad serve, out-of-bound hit, or missed return after bounce.
9. Service Rotation
Serve alternates every 2 points. At 10–10, alternate every point.
10. Doubles
Serve diagonally. Teammates alternate hits.
11. Legal Shot
Hit the ball above the surface from behind the end line. No volleys.
12. Touching the Table
Touching the table with your free hand is a fault
13. Edge Ball
Top edge = good. Side = out. Watch the angles!
14. Change of Ends
Switch sides after each game, and at 5 points in final game.
15. Timeouts
Each player is allowed one timeout per game, lasting 1 minute.
