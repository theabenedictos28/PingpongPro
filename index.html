What is Pingpong?

Ping pong, also known as table tennis, is a dynamic sport that combines speed, skill, and strategy. Originating in the late 19th century as a parlor game in England, it has grown into a competitive sport enjoyed by millions worldwide.

A brief history

Ping pong, also known as table tennis, originated in England during the late 19th century as a parlor game played by the upper class, initially referred to as "whiff-whaff" or "gossima."

1880s: The game is developed in England as a parlor game.
1926: The International Table Tennis Federation (ITTF) is founded.
1988: Ping pong becomes an Olympic sport.

Why choose Pingpong?

Ping pong is not only a fun and exciting sport, but it also offers numerous health benefits, such as improving hand-eye coordination, increasing concentration, and promoting cardiovascular fitness.
