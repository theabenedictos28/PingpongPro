Essential gear every player needs

Paddle

The paddle is used to hit the ball and is made of wood covered with rubber on one or both sides. It greatly influences spin, speed, and control.

Ball

Standard table tennis balls are lightweight (2.7g), 40mm in diameter, and made of plastic. Usually white or orange in color, with a matte finish.

Table

The playing surface is a 9x5 feet table with a consistent bounce. It is divided by a 6-inch-high net in the center.

Net & Post

The net assembly includes the net, posts, and clamps. It should be taut and 6 inches high, stretching across the full table width.

Clothing

Players wear breathable shirts, shorts, and headbands to stay comfortable and perform their best during fast-paced matches.

Shoes

Lightweight, grippy shoes designed for quick lateral movement help players maintain balance and agility on the court.
